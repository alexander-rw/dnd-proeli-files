# Yddragsil, World Tree

Located nearly equidistant to the centre of the major cities within the [City States of Proeli](../City%20States%20of%20Proeli%2019dc7339ad0580c78888ec5051100a21.md) of Proeli, The World Tree is the nexus of nature on the plane as well as the only permanent portal to the [Feywild](../Feywild%201dac7339ad0580b4b408c1947de75496.md). The World tree is visible in most areas of the City States, and on a clear day, can even be seen from Durgam and Ilkazan.

Yddragsil is a huge Bluewood tree, it is the oldest living plant on [The World of Proeli](../../../The%20World%20of%20Proeli%2019dc7339ad058069bc47ce183954e46d.md) and was planted by [Gartax](../../The%20Gods/Gartax%201a0c7339ad0580d19b4bed5a14d751fc.md) (or god of nature if this isn’t right) before the Dawn War. The Canopy Bazaar and the Hall of Verdant Acord sit on one of the lowest branches, approximately a mile off the forest floor. 

## Canopy Bazaar:

High above the earth, nestled within one of the colossal branches of the World Tree **Yddragsil**, sprawls the *Canopy Bazaar*—a vast and wondrous market suspended in the living embrace of the Tree itself. This immense limb, wide as any plain below, supports a teeming hub of trade and culture where the world’s peoples gather beneath the rustling leaves of ancient magic.

The Bazaar is a mosaic of colour and sound, its stalls and pavilions crafted from woven fabrics, shaped bark, and enchanted materials that shimmer in the filtered light. Aromas drift through the air—sweet, spicy, strange—mingling with the clang of forged metal, the hush of whispered deals, and the music of a hundred tongues. Every corner hums with the energy of exchange: of goods, ideas, and stories.

Walkways of grown wood twist through the branch’s contours, linking upper terraces to shaded alcoves below. Mosses and magical lanterns glow softly in the twilight, while above, broad leaves refract sunlight into dazzling patterns across the canopies of tents. Despite the crowds and bustle, the place feels balanced, as if the Tree itself watches and guides the flow of life upon its limb.

## Hall of Verdant Accord:

At the branch’s far end rises the **Hall of Verdant Accord**, a living structure of bark and vine, shaped not built, where delegates from many lands meet. Within its open halls, carved by nature and time, matters of alliance, policy, and law are debated with words as sharp as any blade. Here, amid echoes of old pacts and new ambitions, the fate of many is decided—not by force, but by voice.

## **Faction: The Boughguard**

*“Trade may be loud. Politics may be sharp. But beneath it all, the Tree must endure.”* — Warden-Lorekeeper Aeviraeth

The **Boughguard** are a sacred order entrusted with two interwoven duties: **maintaining peace within the Canopy Bazaar** and **tending to the health and will of Yddragsil**, the World Tree. They are the Tree’s chosen—caretakers, mediators, and, when necessary, the final arbiters of law upon the living branch.

Clad in garb drawn from the forest and arcane grove—woven leaves, bark-forged armor, and crystalline root-glass—the Boughguard stride through the Bazaar not with pomp, but with purpose. They speak softly, act swiftly, and answer to no crown—**only to the Tree.**

### **Origins and Membership**

Founded in the earliest days of the Bazaar, the Boughguard emerged from a coalition of druids, Fey emissaries, and mortal protectors who heard the call of Yddragsil. But as the influence of the City-States grew, a pact was forged: **each State may appoint a single representative to the Boughguard**, ensuring that its stewardship reflects the balance of mortal power as well as the will of nature.

The number of City-State members is always kept **equal**, preventing dominance by any one faction. These appointed guardians must lay aside political loyalty while in service—**swearing to uphold the peace and protect the Tree above all else.**

Alongside them walk the ancient Fey, still-bound to Yddragsil by oath and soul. Together, the mortal and the mystical hold the line between chaos and harmony.

The order is led by the **Circle of Boughs**, a council comprising both elder Fey and seasoned representatives from each City-State. They interpret the Tree’s will and maintain the delicate balance between growth and governance.

### **Roles Within the Boughguard**

- **Peacewalkers** mediate disputes in the Bazaar and act when violence threatens its sanctity.
- **Rootspeakers** commune with the Tree, guiding its growth and channeling its magic.
- **Canopy Sentinels** patrol the heights and shadows, defending against planar threats and unseen dangers.

## **NPCs of the Boughguard**

### **Warden-Lorekeeper Aeviraeth**

*Fey Eladrin | Rootspeaker | Nonbinary (they/them)*

Ethereal and ageless, Aeviraeth moves with the stillness of a deep grove and speaks in a voice that seems to echo with wind and water. Their skin shifts with the seasons—glimmering like frost in winter, flushed with blossoms in spring. As the senior Rootspeaker of the Boughguard, Aeviraeth is said to be able to "listen" to the sap moving through Yddragsil and interpret omens in the curling of its leaves.

Though serene, they hold a quiet power that commands respect—even the most hardened senators grow cautious in their presence. Some whisper they were once a courtier of the Autumn Queen of the Feywild, but their allegiance now lies wholly with the Tree.

> Notable Trait: Never walks directly on the ground; roots rise subtly to meet their feet.
> 
> 
> **Secret**: Aeviraeth has seen visions of a rot deep within Yddragsil, but dares not speak of it—yet.
> 

### **Warden-Captain Cerys Vhalar**

*Human | Peacewalker | Female (she/her)*

Cerys is a former military strategist from Loudwater, appointed as their representative to the Boughguard over a decade ago. Stern but fair, she tempers the more mystical elements of the order with practical leadership and an iron spine. Her armor is polished ironwood reinforced with druidic runes, and she keeps a curved saber at her side—not out of preference, but out of experience.

Though not born to the ways of the Tree, Cerys has come to respect Yddragsil deeply. She sees the Boughguard not as a spiritual order, but as a **sacred responsibility**—a bulwark between greed and harmony.

> Notable Trait: Carries a scroll of oaths, with the signatures of all City-State representatives past and present.
> 
> 
> **Secret**: Cerys is beginning to believe her home state is planning a power grab… and she might have to choose sides.
> 

### Travelling the worldtree

Yddragsil is the *only* permanent anchor portal to the feywild on Proeli - and so people wanting to travel to the feywild will need to:

- Pass a protection test from the **Boughguard** (exact test tbd)
- Crawl through the trunks of the world tree, appearing on the other side in the [Feywild](../Feywild%201dac7339ad0580b4b408c1947de75496.md) itself

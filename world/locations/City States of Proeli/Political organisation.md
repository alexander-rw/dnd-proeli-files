# Political organisation

The Free states are made up of 6 loosely affiliated city state/areas, who all work together to make an overarching law code for the country to use, as well as to combine their power and authority with other countries.

The members of the Collective all send an representative to discussions at the World Tree.

The following cities send a representative:

- Loudwater
- Crossings
- Swifthill
- Frostmark
- A’bel
- Par’sid

If there is a tie in any given vote, the representative of Worm Hill makes the deciding vote.

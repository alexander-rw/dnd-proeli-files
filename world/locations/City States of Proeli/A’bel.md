# A’bel

City in temperate climate, but has dramatic changes through the year.

Summer is super warm and dry, perhaps water sources dry up

Spring is very wet, lakes fill with water, but can cause flooding

Autumn is very windy, hurricanes expected

Winter is very cold, causing water to freeze over.

Houses must be built to withstand all this, and extra work is spent to ensure being able to get through the winter/summer. Dominated by clerics/monks/druids, due to a lack of trust in (arcana?) caster (wizards, warlocks, sorcerors) to address weather related problems.

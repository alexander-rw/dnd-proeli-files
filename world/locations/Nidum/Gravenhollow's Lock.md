# Gravenhollow's Lock

### **Overview:**

Gravenhollow’s Lock is a heavily fortified underground city built atop a massive chasm known as the Yawning Abyss, from which dangerous creatures—aberrations, deep dragons, and other creatures rise. The city’s primary function is to serve as a **watchpost** to protect the rest of Nidum, as well as a place that the martial or magically exceptional of Nidum can earn coin by tracking dangerous monsters.

---

### **Key Details:**

- **Population:** ~4000-5000 (mostly dwarves, gnomes, dragonborn, with a small portion of other races such as elves, goliaths and humans).
- **Government:** Militaristic council led by a Warden-General, elected every ten years from among the city's elite defenders.
- **Primary Purpose:** Defense and containment of threats from the Underdark. All citizens are trained in combat or support roles.

---

### **Key Locations:**

1. **The Deepgate Bastion**
    
    A fortress spanning the entire chasm. Contains ballistae, arcane wards, and rotating guard posts. The gate itself is a huge construct of runed steel that can be sealed magically and physically.
    
2. **The Emberforge**
    
    A magma-fed forge run by fire genasi, producing specialised arms. Magical components are infused here to ward against magic attacks.
    
3. **The Veinspire**
    
    A stalagmite-turned-tower that houses the city’s arcanists and priests. Contains the **Seal Codex**, a magical record of all known breaches and creatures that have attempted to rise.
    
4. **The Vault of Silence**
    
    A deep prison where aberrations, doppelgangers, or aberrant cultists are held. Guarded by enchanted stone golems and magic wards.
    
    ### **The Power Beneath: The Heartshard Core**
    
    At the centre of the **Throat of the Deep**, encased in layers of crystal and obsidian, pulses the **Heartshard Core**—an ancient, quasi-living power source that, known to very few, is the Knowledge nexus as well as a source of power for most of Nidum. Most that know of its existence claim it a fissure into a plane of pure elemental energy.
    
    It emits vast amounts of raw arcana in fluctuating waves. Gravenhollow’s Lock was built directly over it, both to tap its power and to serve as the first and last line of defense should anything emerge from the breach surrounding it.
    

---

### **Unique Defenses & Traits:**

- **Runestone Lattice:** Enchants the cavern walls with a lattice of runes that suppress teleportation and psionic intrusion, as well as strengthening the cavern that the city is built in.

---

## **Adventure Hooks:**

- A new kind of deep creature has breached the wards—one that speaks with the voices of lost loved ones.
- A traitor among the city's elite is sabotaging the defenses to allow something from the Throat to ascend.
- The Seal Codex is slowly erasing entries, suggesting forgotten horrors are returning.
- **Arcane Blackout**: A sudden drop in power causes defenses to fail as something from the Throat begins to rise. Was it sabotage or the Core resisting the siphoning?
- **Prophet of the Deep**: A child born near the Bleeding Wall begins speaking in an unknown language, giving warnings of “the awakening below.”
- **Vel Arcanis Demands More**: Surface nobles demand more energy, putting Gravenhollow at greater risk. Do the players side with the surface, or protect the Lock?
- **Corruption in the Soulbinders**: A growing number of engineers are showing signs of mutation and collective thought—possibly indicating an emerging hive mind.

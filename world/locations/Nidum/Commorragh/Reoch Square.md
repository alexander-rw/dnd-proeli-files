# Reoch Square

The central square of the city, Reoch Square is signified with its smaller status of Reoch - the stone statue that holds the city up - depicted in miniature within the centre. The square is bustling, busy, and has a direct line to the announcement tower, so most folk gather here when announcements are being made by the Hand. 

It is also the location of the Hangman, where traitors are hung in public.

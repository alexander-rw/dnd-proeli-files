# Commorragh

Capital City of the Magocracy of [Nidum](../Nidum%2019dc7339ad0580caa4e3c9f4df3b360a.md), Comorragh is located high in the Ephyran mountains - a mountain range that spans the majority of the island of Nidum. 

A sprawling, busy city, Comorragh is built upon a large rocky plateau. The plateau is held up by a large stone statue of a humanoid, known as **Reoth,** holding the city up upon its shoulders. There is a metal platform to lift things from the base of the statue to the city proper.

### A fascist mage state

The streets of Commorragh gleam with enchanted splendour, but beneath this facade lurks an unease as shapeshifting liquid metal automatons, known as the Keepers, patrol every corner.  Their mercurial forms allow them to infiltrate any gathering, assume any identity, and eliminate any whisper of dissent with terrifying efficiency. Your bowl of fruit could be a keeper. Your cat could be a Keeper. Your own mother could be a Keeper.

These sentinels embody the regime's omnipresent surveillance, while the city’s announcement tower glorifies the manifest destiny of Nidum as the most magically capable city on the planet.

Public displays of loyalty are mandatory, with grand magical parades showcasing the might of Nidum's arcane legions, while those deemed “antagonistic” are relegated to labour camps, exiled, or worst of all - hung in the main square of Comorragh if the Hand so much as heard a whisper of dissent.

### Keepers

The city is protected by liquid metal automatons known as “Keepers”. Keepers are able to shapeshift into any shape, mimic faces and voices, and morph their limbs into weapons - making them incredibly dangerous both covertly and in head on battle.

The keepers were created hundreds of years ago by the Hand, after a coup attempting to rid them of them shortly before they were made.

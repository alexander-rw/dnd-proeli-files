# The Inkpot

**Proprietor**: **Maelo**, an old, partially blind human woman whose fingers are stained with ink. She has perfect tactile memory and "feels" the shape of maps with her hands.

**Description**: Located near a switchback stair on the first tier of the **Hua Shan**, this cliff-clinging wooden building overlooks the floating docks below. Inside, ink bottles sit on shelves, and maps cover every surface—nautical charts, sea trenches, volcanic vents, and ancient ruins. 

**Services**:

- Custom maps (land, sea, or sunken ruin)
- Charting of magical anomalies or leyline crossings
- Commissions for treasure maps (with steep fees for secrecy)
- Sales of magical ink (used in scrolls, scribing, or for marking planar charts)

# Major Theobald Ursa

The major (LG male human [veteran](https://5e.tools/bestiary.html#veteran_mm)) is a huge, barrel-chested man with a bright red beard and a circlet of thin red-gray hair on his balding head. His usual garb is a gaudy red and yellow kilt with his warhammer and dagger hanging from a broad leather belt. He speaks with a cockney accent, deep voiced.

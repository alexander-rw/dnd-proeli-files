### **Scrap Dive**

- **Task**: Dive into a nearby ship graveyard to recover usable materials from a ruined galley. Avoid sea scavengers and rival salvagers.
- **Obstacles**: Poor visibility, territorial sharks, undead sailors still haunting the wreck.

### 2. **Anchor Chains and Blood Debts**

- **Task**: Convince or pressure a dock gang to return anchor chain and rigging they stole during an earlier conflict with the NPC.
- **Twist**: The gang has a blood pact with a sea hag—break the curse or fight through it.

### 3. **Cargo Escort**

- **Task**: Escort a load of refurbished timber and brass fittings through the city’s criminally dense floating canals. Thieves' Guilds want a piece.
- **Twist**: One of the crates contains a stowaway child with ties to a noble house.

### 4. **Whalebone Black Market**

- **Task**: Buy rare whalebone for reinforcing the hull from a shady merchant operating on an old sea platform. Bring coin, but keep your weapons handy.
- **Complication**: The merchant is being hunted by sea elves who believe the whalebone trade is a crime against their gods.

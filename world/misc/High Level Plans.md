# High Level Plans

- 1000 years after current campaigns - safe inspectors and Verderra.
- This PMP came to be as a result of the Dawn War. In the fighting of that war, parts of various gods fell into this PMP and embedded themselves into the fabric of the plane. These embedment sites are called ‘nexus points’.
- The Gods’ influence are focused around a their individual nexus point. These nexus’ tend to have a conceptual underpinning

Nexus names: 

- Knowledge - The Weaver
    - Magocracy?
        - This nexus is by itself on an island
- Drive - Kord
    - Ideal Meritocracy - power based purely on talent, and if you are too good at something, you have to prove it
    - Oligarchy (a small group of people having control of a country or organization)
- Nature - Gartax
    - Radical democracy (worms get votes)
    - A set of city states who are consistently fighting with the monarchy which controls order over the ‘talent’ nexus.
- Sentience - the enchantment artefact
    - Technocracy - shadowrun or equivalent system run over there rather than DnD
        - The enchantment item ([Ultimate items ](Ultimate%20items%2019dc7339ad0580c69b7ed94ebf9d6d38.md)) is housed here, and is the source of the sentience of the city itself.
        - This nexus is far away from the others, on the other side of the world
- Order - The Raven Queen
    - Ilkazan, ruled by the Varian dynasty
- Death - Alex’s Shadow Fiend
    - This nexus has never been located and the existence of it is largely a folk tale. It ‘apparently’ exists out somewhere in the Lost Lands.
    - The Knowledge Magocracy, Life Meritocracy, and Life Monarchy are all interested in finding this nexus and have sent explorers to search for it.
- Freedom - Karion
    - this nexus is in the sea and it’s avatar has make the decision not to directly meddle in mortal affairs. Consequently, it’s aura attracts those who do not subscribe to the established order

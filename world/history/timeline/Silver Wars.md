# Silver Wars

Year (AC): 301
Month: (6) Highsummer (Summer)
Day: 19
Location(s) (links): Nidum

A rich vein of magically enchanted copper was found in the Ephyran mountain range, leading the local Duke to become extremely wealthy. The wealth later led this person, [Duke Bifund Rexdelver](../../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Duke%20Bifund%20Rexdelver%2023dc7339ad05800c9a5ddde0b47762fd.md)  to rebel against the Hand, winning several smaller scale battles before being captured and publicly boiled alive in molten copper. The duke and his family were all killed as a reminder of the power of the state.

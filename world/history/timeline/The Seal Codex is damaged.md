# The Seal Codex is damaged

Year (AC): 147
Month: (12) Frostmarch (Winter)
Day: 14
Location(s) (links): Nidum

The Seal Codex is damaged during an earthquake in Comorroagh/Gravenhollow. This results in a number of creatures erupting from the ground and damaging both cities, as well as ravaging the plains, mountains and seas around Comorragh. A member of the hand, Juliana de Mowbray, is killed during these events

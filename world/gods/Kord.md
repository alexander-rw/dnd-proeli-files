# Kord

Nexus: Drive nexus, between the monarchy and the free states 

### Drive Nexus description:

The drive nexus is suspended above a completely tranquil lake situated on the boundary of the monarchy and the free states. The nexus itself takes the form of a tornado-like lightning storm, which consistently undulates and twirls in the air about a earthen platform which is suspended a mile over the lake itself. This platform can be reached by one of the eight earthen spurs which start at the edge of the lake and then reach up into the sky to connect with the platform.

Both countries have sought to take command of the drive nexus at various points in their history. However, for now there is a general truce concerning this area, and the platform is considered a neutral zone between the two countries. Any issues regarding actions on the platform are dealt with by a special dual law code, which requires the rulers of both groups to be present during the presentation of the facts and verdict.

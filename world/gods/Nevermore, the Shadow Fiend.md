# Nevermore, the Shadow Fiend

## Domains

- Death
- Necromancy
- Soul collection

## Original Lore

It is said that the Shadow Fiend has the soul of a poet, and in fact he has thousands of them. Over the ages he has claimed the souls of poets, priests, emperors, beggars, slaves, philosophers, criminals and (naturally) heroes; no sort of soul escapes him. What he does with them is unknown. No one has ever peered into the Abysm whence Nevermore reaches out like an eel from among astral rocks. Does he devour them one after another? Does he mount them along the halls of an eldritch temple, or pickle the souls in necromantic brine? Is he merely a puppet, pushed through the dimensional rift by a demonic puppeteer? Such is its evil, so intense its aura of darkness, that no rational mind may penetrate it. Of course, if you really want to know where the stolen souls go, there's one sure way to find out: Add your soul to its collection. Or just wait for Nevermore.

## Appearance

Nevermore appears as a dark, demonic humanoid-shaped creature. Its hands are large and clawed, its skin a deep blood red bordering on black, and its body terminates in a single point, shrouded in shadow. It moves by floating across the ground, while dragging its hands to pick up speed and turn.

## Other information

- Nevermore the Shadow Fiend is a member of the Court of Ristul, a group of demon lords which lend their strength to someone and in exchange, they must swear The Oath. The only thing known about the Oath is that it raises the dead.

Unbeknownst to the civilizations of the known world, Nevermore is the architect behind the shadow incursions of Ilkazan. The incursions, where shadowy entities emerge to wreak havoc on any who are unlucky enough to come across them, have long been considered a natural blight or the work of ancient curses. However, these events are the direct consequence of Nevermore’s power seeping into the material plane, his influence stretching like tendrils through the cracks of reality. Those who fall victim to the incursions often find their souls inexplicably drawn into oblivion, claimed by Nevermore to fuel his insatiable hunger. The true nature of these incursions remains unknown.

### The Domain of Nevermore

Nevermore’s domain is the Abysm, a layer of the abyss of perpetual shadow and despair. The Abysm is said to be an endless expanse filled with jagged astral rocks, swirling darkness, and echoes of the tortured screams of claimed souls. It is a place where time and space lose meaning, and the boundaries between life and death blur. The souls residing in the Abysm are said to endure eternal torment, their energies siphoned to sustain Nevermore’s power.

Shadow Fiend’s influence extends beyond the Abysm into the material world, particularly through his followers. Cults dedicated to Nevermore are rare but extremely dangerous. These cultists often operate in secret, seeking to spread shadows wherever they go. They believe that through shadow, true understanding and enlightenment can be reached, as it strips away the illusions of light and order.

Shadow Fiend is responsible for a lot of the creation of weapons that demons from the blood war use. His secondary aim, other than collecting souls to create shadow spawn, is to corrupt powerful crafters to create weapons for him 

### Religious Practices and Followers

Followers of Nevermore, often referred to as the "Binders of Souls," partake in dark rituals to honor their master and strengthen his presence. Among their practices are:

- **Soul Offering:** Cultists capture and sacrifice individuals, offering their souls to Nevermore. These rituals involve chanting ancient hymns in a forgotten tongue, inscribing runes of darkness, and invoking the shadowy tendrils of the Abysm to claim the victim.
- **Shadow Communion:** Followers meditate in complete darkness for days at a time, seeking to connect with Nevermore. Those who succeed claim to hear whispers of forbidden knowledge or even glimpse the edges of the Abysm.
- **The Veil of Shadows:** Cultists perform a rite where they cast a shroud of shadow over a town or village, plunging it into unnatural darkness in order to make the inhabitants more likely to
- **The Oath of Oblivion:** High-ranking followers swear an unbreakable oath to Nevermore, offering their own souls in servitude. In return, they are granted necromantic power and the ability to raise the dead as soulless servants.

Nevermore’s second in command is an undead earth genasi paladin called Janshai Fortbreaker. Janshai’s main focus is to raise undead armies in nevermore’s wake, and acting as a military general for the shadowspawn.

Among mortals, Nevermore has made a pact with the [Zubelia Zubelia](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Zubelia%20Zubelia%201e4c7339ad0580dab0d5ff5b4be11587.md). A figure of shadowy renown, spreads Nevermore’s influence subtly, gaining power not through armies or open conquest but through fear, manipulation, and the quiet corruption of individuals.

# Nayla Moonwillow

Mother of [Micaiah L. Eynes](../Player%20Characters/Micaiah%20L%20Eynes%201dac7339ad05801bb96afca6ffc4b7ee.md) 

Occupation: she was a town planner, helping organise things within the town such as construction, logistics and similar. She is not bad at her job, but used her position of power to abuse the populace by forcing her workers to do things for her that exhaust them. 

Part of [The Moonwillows](The%20Moonwillows%201dac7339ad05804eba66c4a1cafb6365.md).

Whereabouts currently unknown.

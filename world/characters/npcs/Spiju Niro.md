# Spiju Niro

**Appearance:** Even descriptions of Spiju Niro vary wildly. Some claim they are a lithe half-elf with piercing dark eyes that seem to see through any deception. Others describe a more stout, human-like figure who favors heavy cloaks and masks that obscure their features. This ambiguity is likely intentional, a tool to sow confusion and maintain their anonymity. They are rumored to possess several disguises and personas, allowing them to move freely throughout the city without detection.

**Personality and Methods:** Spiju Niro is known for their cunning and patience. They prefer to manipulate events from the shadows, using their network of informants and agents to achieve their goals. Direct confrontation is a last resort. They value information above all else, and their network of spies extends to all levels of Durgam society, from dockworkers to minor officials and even, some whisper, within the ranks of the other factions.

**Potential Interactions with the Players:**

- The players might be hired by Spiju Niro for a delicate task, perhaps involving infiltration, information gathering, or the retrieval of a stolen item.
- They could find themselves in opposition to the Shadow Koi Guild, perhaps investigating one of their criminal enterprises or protecting someone they have targeted.
- Spiju Niro might become a recurring antagonist, a shadowy figure pulling strings behind the scenes.
- The players might even be approached by rivals of Spiju Niro, seeking their help in undermining the Guild's power.

Hideout: Located in a seemingly unassuming tea house in one of the older, more labyrinthine floating sections. The tea house, called the **"Misty Kettle,"** has a hidden entrance leading to a network of underground and underwater tunnels where the Shadow Koi Guild conducts its business. Spiju Niro is a master of disguise and information, always seeking to expand their influence. They might offer the party lucrative (and potentially dangerous) jobs or sell valuable secrets. The tea house is only open from 8am - 4pm, but guild members can access the hideout through a small hidden access through the stone at the back of the building, down a side alley.

# Eight Stars Dancing

**Eight Stars Dancing**

- **Name:** Eight Stars Dancing
- **Race:** Goliath
- **Accent**: Vaguely eastern european
- **Languages:** Goblin, Giant, Common, Orcish
- **Profession:** Ship Captain (currently grounded)
    - Ship: [**Sea Serpent's Sigh**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/vehicles/keelboat)
- **Description:** Eight Stars Dancing is a skinny goliath with deep grey mottled skin. His once-proud captain's coat is now slightly frayed, and a worried frown often creases his features.
- As he greets the characters, He speaks with a slow, stoic energy as he recounts the tale of his ship's misfortune – a run-in with a monstrous green dragon that left his vessel, heavily damaged and currently languishing in one of Durgam's smaller, barely functional dry docks. He is desperate to find the funds for repairs and regain his former glory on the waves. He might offer the party passage or information in exchange for assistance.

# The Moonwillows

Moon/sea elf family

Secretly a family of evil magic practicioners, who select a few members of the population of the town (~8000 people, they choose 3-4 / yr) in a ‘selection contest’ every year to ‘help on a pilgrimage to the feywild’. In reality, they sacrifice one person (usually from the town) once a year to be able to keep their youth and beauty. 

Members of the family:

Head of the Family - **Maven Moonwillow**

- Nonbinary sea elf,

Protector of the family - **Owen Moonwillow (neé Aegwyn).** 

- **Ginger haired, bearded moon elf. Paladin of Devotion (lvl9).**

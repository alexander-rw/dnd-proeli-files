# The Hand

Rulers of the Capital City of the Magocracy of [Nidum](../Locations/Nidum%2019dc7339ad0580caa4e3c9f4df3b360a.md) 

As of 333 AC, they are made up of the following members:

- [**Quinn Thea**](The%20Hand/Quinn%20Thea%201f6c7339ad0580398e39ec529a21355f.md), An Aasimar Cleric of the Weaver (he/him)
    - Annointed 271AC
- [Paige Jare](The%20Hand/Paige%20Jare%201f6c7339ad0580bcb34efee3d37982df.md) , A Hobgoblin Divination Wizard (she/her)
    - Annointed 309AC
- [Rhendron Stonespine](The%20Hand/Rhendron%20Stonespine%201f6c7339ad058072a277cc48ba734587.md), A Goliath Sorcerer (Runechild) (they/them).
    - Annointed 307AC
- [Tezzerret](The%20Hand/Tezzerret%201f6c7339ad058046a2b6e5ba06ddb075.md), A Human Artificer (he/him)
    - Annointed 6AC
- [*Maglocunus*](The%20Hand/Maglocunus%201f6c7339ad0580ebb251e4d026da765c.md), a Blue Dragonborn Lore Bard and **Caomhnóir** of the Sacred Library (she/her)
    - Annointed 89AC
    - **Caomhnoir,** pronounced ****keev-nar, is an honorary title for the protector of the sacred library.

Of the five current members: 

- The only person that knows Tezzerret’s true age is Maglocunus
- Maglocunus is the only non-humanoid of the five hand members
- Rhendron is the latest in the line of “Runechildren”, a select magic school that only humanoids born of the Ephyran mountains are able to tap into. It’s nearly always been the case that at least one of the hand have been a Runechild - since records began hundreds of years ago

The members of the hand must be: Bard, Wizard, Artificer, Cleric, Sorcerer, or Paladin

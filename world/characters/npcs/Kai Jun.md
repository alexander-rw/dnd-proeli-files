# Kai Jun

Description: (they/them) ~5ft6, water genasi, flawless blue skin, deep pink eyes with obsidian coloured irises.  

Accent: soft spoken, gentle voiced english. not posh, not cockney.

A water genasi, and organiser of [The Drowned Arena](../Locations/The%20Drowned%20Arena%201efc7339ad05800496dfcc794758575f.md), Kai is very matter of fact - and won’t take any shit from anyone, especially not within the arena. Many find them a little abrasive - until you get to know them, and find out they’re just very straightforward. 

Kai is lean, wiry, and covered in old battle scars and ritual tattoos that glow faintly underwater. Their long hair is braided and dipped in crimson coral dust. Once a pirate who lost everything in a doomed coup, he found religion of a dark sort beneath the waves and now claims *the pit is the truest god left in the world.*

"In the pit, there is no mercy. Only tide and teeth.”

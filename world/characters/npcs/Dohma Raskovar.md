**Description**: Orcish Rogue, tall, male, lithe. Accent: gruff, londonish.

Leader of the [Ivory Throne (mercenary band)](Ivory%20Throne%20(mercenary%20band)%201efc7339ad05804aad72eaded63fa687.md). Can often be found in the [The Hanging Tree](../Locations/The%20Hanging%20Tree%201efc7339ad0580f192b6d9934be180e3.md). 

Dohma Raskovar is the latest in a long line of leaders of the Ivory Throne, a band of mercenaries. Under Raskovar's leadership, the throne has grown to over a hundred strong, and now they handle multiple contracts simultaneously, occasionally banding together to fight as a single unit in battle.

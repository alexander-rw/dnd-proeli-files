# Backstory

Droo comes from a town that he doesn’t know the name of - as he got lost at age 7. 

Things he remembers about the town: 

- There were deciduous trees throughout the area, so not near a tropical area.
- It was a very small town of mostly dwarves and humans. It was mostly a farming town, and a small stream ran through it
- It was mostly rolling hills nearby, although a larger, different forest wasn’t more than a few days travel away. The trees in that forest were… strange.

Droo’s family (all dwarven):

- Cool aunt: Dolores.
- Mother: Latetia Burlymore. Dwarven woman, good with crops and herbs. Town apothecary.
- Father: Rinn Burlymore. Shopkeep of the apothecary. Also does some farming.
- Both parents born and grew up in that town.
- Siblings:
    - Younger sister: Diana Burlymore.
        - 4 in 326 AC (born 322 AC)
    - Older brother: Dolo Burlymore. He’s the goody-two-shoes of the family, so has a bit of a rivalry with Droo.
        - 14 in 326 AC (born 312 AC)
    - Older brother: John Blythe Burlymore. Shopkeep.
        - 22 at 326 AC (born 304 AC)
    - Older brother: Lionel Burlymore - A level 1 ranger, and helper at the shop. Him and Latetia would mostly collect herbs in the forest.
        - 24 at 326 AC (born 302 AC)

The Feywild:

- At the age of 7, Droo got lost in the woods - and wandered into the Feywild. After walking around for a couple of days, and starving, droo smelled the aroma of freshly baked cookies coming from a nearby cave - Eileen. She took him in and fed him - for a cost. He had to help her keep where she lived tidy, and stocked.
- Droo was lost and alone, save for Eileen’s company. He had to forage for herbs while being watched by the Meenlocks that Eileen controlled, and had to clean and maintain Eileen’s Creeping Cave. He was scared that the Meenlocks would attack him, or that Eileen would shout at him for doing something wrong.
- Droo’s only friend for years was a Pixie, called Pip - short for Pipamixulorous - who often helped Droo forage while he was in the feywild, and taught him Sylvan.
- When Droo was 10, Eileen went off on a journey - and came back with a friend for Droo. Gretchen was a couple of years younger than Droo, but they hit it off. He had a friend to forage with, and someone to share his company with.

After years, while out on a foraging trip, Droo and Gretchen found a portal that led somewhere. While Droo immediately wanted to jump in, Gretchen was a little more cautious. Droo jumped in - but not before promising gretchen that, if he made it out alive the other side, he’d come back and get her.

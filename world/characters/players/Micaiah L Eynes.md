# Backstory

Dad: [Milton K. Eynes](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Milton%20K%20Eynes%201dac7339ad0580ef9ab3cec45e595b44.md) (dad was ~30s when Micaiah was born, ~76 when he died of old age)

Mum: [Nayla Moonwillow](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Nayla%20Moonwillow%201dac7339ad05804ca25ce08bd6546b7c.md) (current whereabouts unknown)

Micaiah grew up in the town of [Gallowmere](../Locations/City%20States%20of%20Proeli/Gallowmere%201dac7339ad05803aafd1d57941d93a35.md). His parents met shortly before he was born, but his mother Nayla ran away when Micaiah was very young. 

Growing up, Micaiah spent most of his time with his father helping to run cons on the rich folk of the nearby more affluent town, [Loudwater](../Locations/City%20States%20of%20Proeli/Loudwater%201dac7339ad0580f3aa78c4ac7c9e3b9f.md). As his father aged, he took this mantle up himself. Over the years, the two took significant amounts of gold from the nobles of the city and turned it over to the less fortunate of both Loudwater and Gallowmere. 

As Milton got older, Micaiah ended up taking a lot of the burden on of looking after the townsfolk and ‘liberating’ gold from nobles in the town to 

One day Micaiah decided to go to [Loudwater](../Locations/City%20States%20of%20Proeli/Loudwater%201dac7339ad0580f3aa78c4ac7c9e3b9f.md) to see the celebration of the Shining Cliffs. His father was too old to travel, so Micaiah decided to travel without him. When Micaiah returned, his father had passed away due to his age. 

Micaiah left the town shortly after, and has been travelling ever since.

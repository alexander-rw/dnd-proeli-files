# Backstory

Born in 306 AC, Clarissa is the oldest of three children. A descendant and member of [Forge Dasolys - Tiefling Forging Family](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Forging%20Families%20of%20Hawksreach/Forge%20Dasolys%20-%20Tiefling%20Forging%20Family%201dac7339ad05808c9656d218076e1656.md), well known for creating weapons for the royal family in [Hawksreach](../Locations/Ilkazan/Hawksreach%201d8c7339ad05800ab67ce21a7b660b04.md), Clarissa is a forge cleric and worshipper of the god of crafting, [Gartax](../The%20Gods/Gartax%201a0c7339ad0580d19b4bed5a14d751fc.md).

Clarissa has two twin brothers, [Zeron Dasolys](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Forging%20Families%20of%20Hawksreach/Forge%20Dasolys%20-%20Tiefling%20Forging%20Family/Zeron%20Dasolys%201dac7339ad05801c91c7ebc7c6b9ede5.md) & [Dave Dasolys](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Forging%20Families%20of%20Hawksreach/Forge%20Dasolys%20-%20Tiefling%20Forging%20Family/Dave%20Dasolys%201dac7339ad058079a3bdd50f46c15499.md). Clarissa’s parents are [Velrissa Dasolys](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Forging%20Families%20of%20Hawksreach/Forge%20Dasolys%20-%20Tiefling%20Forging%20Family/Velrissa%20Dasolys%201dac7339ad05809dac50df95fdeb69cd.md) (mother) and [Maurut Dasolys](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Forging%20Families%20of%20Hawksreach/Forge%20Dasolys%20-%20Tiefling%20Forging%20Family/Maurut%20Dasolys%201dac7339ad0580979ddcfa7315ff7a3e.md) (father). Both born on the same day (again on the comet anniversary) in AC 275, Velrissa and Maurut were originally part of rival forging families - The Dasolys’ and the Carakas’ - the two fell in love while young and ended up uniting both families under a single banner. 

Maurut, a forge cleric and devout worshipper of [Gartax](../The%20Gods/Gartax%201a0c7339ad0580d19b4bed5a14d751fc.md), priorities worship and creation above his wife. Velrissa, while also a worshipper, has wanted to expand the forge and make a name for the family for a long time, after having lost numerous ceremonial forging contracts to the likes of [Forge Khazgur - Orcish Forging Family](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Forging%20Families%20of%20Hawksreach/Forge%20Khazgur%20-%20Orcish%20Forging%20Family%201dac7339ad05807b8d0ffba2a1f4d75a.md) and [Forge Solinnor - Dwarven Forging Family](../Non%20Player%20Characters%20(NPCs)/Forging%20Families%20of%20Hawksreach/Forge%20Solinnor%20-%20Dwarven%20Forging%20Family%201dac7339ad05803c95abe44b1c9ade94.md). 

After a long time spent in Hawksreach, Clarissa decides to head out and travel. She is tired of the daily routine of life, and tired that her parents often argue and fight over the state of the forge and their differing views. 

Clarissa wants to find a special ore to craft something for her parents - both as a rite of pilgrimage, and as a way of re-igniting the love of craft between them again.
